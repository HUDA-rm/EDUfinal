Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right coach for me?

Browse our coaches' expertise tags. Whether you're focused on UI/UX, Frontend, or Databases, each coach has a specific area of mastery to guide you effectively.

Are sessions live or recorded?

Most sessions are live to ensure real-time feedback, but they are also recorded for your future reference and review.

Do I get a certificate after completion?

Yes! Upon successful completion of your learning journey and projects, you'll receive a verified certificate to showcase on LinkedIn.

Do I need prior design experience?

Not at all. Our coaches start from the basics of Figma and design thinking, helping you build your portfolio from scratch even without any art background.

What Frontend frameworks are covered?

We focus on the most in-demand technologies including React.js, Next.js, and Tailwind CSS, ensuring you're ready for the modern job market.

Will I learn both SQL and NoSQL?

Yes, our database track covers relational databases like PostgreSQL and NoSQL options like MongoDB for a full-stack understanding of data.

Do you provide job placement assistance?

We offer intensive "Finding a job" workshops, portfolio reviews, and technical mock interviews to maximize your chances of landing a role.

Can I switch between learning paths?

Absolutely! We encourage flexibility. You can discuss your goals with your coach and adjust your curriculum to include more design or more backend as you grow.
